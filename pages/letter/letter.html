For my Dearest Elin♥,

In whispers of night, where stars softly gleam,
I scribe these words, a lover's tender dream.
Though distance steals me from your embrace,
Know, my love, I long for your grace.

Alas, this year's journey seems denied,
Yet my heart's compass still by your side.
With every breath, with every sigh,
Your essence, my love, shall never die.

Forgive the hands of time, its cruel jest,
That separates us from the love confessed.
But fear not, my darling, for in its stead,
An alternate day, just for us two.

We'll weave a tapestry of moments bright,
Underneath the moon's enchanting light.
Each kiss, each laugh, a symphony rare,
Our love's song, filling the evening air.

So hold onto hope, my sweet Elin fair,
For soon, my love, I shall be there.
Until then, let this verse gently convey,
My unwavering love, come what may.

Yours always and forever,
Areg♥
